# ASRS Requirements Reference — AASB S1 & S2

Australian Sustainability Reporting Standards — aligned with IFRS ISSB S1 & S2.
Mandatory for in-scope Australian entities under the Treasury Laws Amendment Act.

> ⚠️ Always verify current status, thresholds, and guidance via web search.
> These requirements are subject to ongoing AASB updates and ASIC guidance notes.

## AASB S1 — General Requirements {#s1}

### Core Objective
Require entities to disclose information about sustainability-related risks and opportunities that
could reasonably be expected to affect cash flows, access to finance, or cost of capital.

### Key Disclosure Areas

| Code | Requirement | Type |
|---|---|---|
| S1.6–S1.9 | Governance — Board oversight of sustainability risks | Narrative |
| S1.10–S1.13 | Governance — Management's role | Narrative |
| S1.14–S1.24 | Strategy — Risks & opportunities identified | Narrative + data |
| S1.25–S1.36 | Strategy — Effect on business model & value chain | Narrative |
| S1.37–S1.44 | Risk Management — Processes for identifying & managing risks | Narrative |
| S1.45–S1.55 | Metrics & Targets — Performance metrics | Data + narrative |

## AASB S2 — Climate-related Disclosures {#s2}

### Core Objective
Require disclosure of information about climate-related risks and opportunities, enabling users
to understand an entity's exposure and response to climate change.

### Governance Pillar

| Code | Requirement | Data/Narrative |
|---|---|---|
| S2.6–S2.9 | Board oversight of climate-related risks & opportunities | Narrative |
| S2.10–S2.13 | Management's role in climate risk governance | Narrative |
| S2.14 | Board skills & competencies in climate matters | Narrative |

### Strategy Pillar

| Code | Requirement | Data/Narrative |
|---|---|---|
| S2.15–S2.20 | Climate-related risks & opportunities affecting entity | Narrative + data |
| S2.21 | Current and anticipated effects on business model | Narrative |
| S2.22–S2.24 | Climate resilience — scenario analysis | Narrative + data |
| S2.25 | Transition planning | Narrative |

**Scenario Analysis requirements (S2.22–S2.24):**
- Must use climate scenarios including a ≤1.5°C scenario
- Disclose time horizons: short (≤1yr), medium (1–5yr), long (>5yr)
- Consider physical and transition risks under each scenario

### Risk Management Pillar

| Code | Requirement | Data/Narrative |
|---|---|---|
| S2.26 | Process for identifying climate-related risks & opportunities | Narrative |
| S2.27 | Process for assessing and prioritising climate risks | Narrative |
| S2.28 | Integration into overall risk management | Narrative |

### Metrics & Targets Pillar

| Code | Requirement | Unit |
|---|---|---|
| S2.29(a) | Scope 1 GHG emissions | tCO₂e |
| S2.29(b) | Scope 2 GHG emissions (location & market-based) | tCO₂e |
| S2.29(c) | Scope 3 GHG emissions (if material) | tCO₂e |
| S2.29(d) | GHG emissions intensity | tCO₂e per $ or unit |
| S2.30 | Climate-related transition risks — financial quantification | $M |
| S2.31 | Climate-related physical risks — financial quantification | $M |
| S2.32 | Capital deployment towards climate opportunities | $M |
| S2.33 | Internal carbon price (if used) | $/tCO₂e |
| S2.34 | Remuneration linked to climate metrics | Narrative |
| S2.35–S2.38 | Climate-related targets | Narrative + data |

**GHG Measurement requirements:**
- Must follow GHG Protocol or equivalent internationally recognised standard
- Disclose measurement approach, inputs, assumptions
- State if prior period data has been restated

## Phased Implementation {#phasing}

| Group | Entity Criteria (meet 2 of 3) | Mandatory From |
|---|---|---|
| Group 1 | Assets >$5B OR Revenue >$1B OR Employees >500 | FY commencing 1 Jan 2025 |
| Group 2 | Assets >$1B OR Revenue >$500M OR Employees >250 | FY commencing 1 Jan 2026 |
| Group 3 | Assets >$500M OR Revenue >$200M OR Employees >100 | FY commencing 1 Jan 2027 |

**Phased relief available:**
- Scope 3 — 1 year relief for all groups from first reporting year
- Scenario analysis — modified approach permitted in first year
- Comparative information — relief in first year

> ⚠️ Thresholds and relief provisions subject to change — verify with current AASB guidance.

## Key Definitions {#definitions}

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Climate-related risk** | Physical risk (acute/chronic) or transition risk (policy, legal, technology, market, reputational) |
| **Reporting boundary** | Scope of entities included — typically operational or financial control |
| **Material** | Information is material if omitting or misstating it could influence user decisions |
| **Value chain** | Upstream and downstream activities related to the entity's operations |
| **Transition plan** | Aspects of strategy and business model relating to climate transition |
