## Reporting Requirement

To claim **"in accordance with GRI Standards"**:
1. Report all GRI 2 disclosures (with limited exemptions)
2. Report GRI 3-1, 3-2, and 3-3 for each material topic
3. Report at least one Topic Standard disclosure for each material topic

For **"GRI-referenced"** (less stringent): report any GRI disclosures without full coverage.
